Introduction:-
Rainbow shows up as a spectrum of light: a band of familiar colors that include red, orange, yellow, green, blue, and violet. Many scientists, however, think "indigo" is too close to blue to be truly distinguishable. VIBGYOR is the easiest way to remember the colors of rainbow. VIBGYORstands for-
V-Violet
I-Indigo
B-Blue
G-Green
Y-Yellow
O-Orange
R-Red
White light is how our eyes perceive all the colors of the rainbow mixed together. Sunlight appears white. When sunlight hits a rain droplet, some of the light is reflected. The electromagnetic spectrum is made of light with many different wavelengths, and each is reflected at a different angle. Thus, spectrum is separated, producing a rainbow.
